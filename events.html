Waterford Events

Winterval

Explore christmas time in waterford through the variety of family orientated events and workshops that Winterval has to offer. Get skating with waterford on ice, watch the heart of waterford city come to life with christmas lights, the christmas crafts festival, markets and the visit of santa amd his elves. Plenty of fun for all the family!

Throughout the parade you will get to witness a wide variety of acts from dancing to martial arts along with the many colorful floats that are made by different clubs and societys just for this special day.

Harvest Festival

A festival celebrating food heritage and culture in Waterford! Take a day out with the whole family and watch the streets of waterford come to life through the wide variety of food available, from a variety of countries around the world.

Oktoberfest Beag

A festival designed for adults of all ages to explore all the best things about Bavaria including the food, music, atmosphere and of course beer! Discover and embrace your inner German.

Artbeat

A completely free air music festival held in the viking triangle of waterford every saturday during the summer. Explore the creativity of waterford and see all the glories of art it has to offer.
