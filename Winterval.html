Explore Waterford

Winterval

Winterval Lights

Colourful festive Light hung around inner city Waterford

Come and see the festival lights all around the inner city, enjoy the annual christmas light show (large projector shines christmas themed lightshow and music onto wall).

Waterford Eye

Ferris wheel on the Quay

Gives a view over Waterford from the top of the Eye, offers a VIP Package for 30mins ride time, blankets, chocolates and a bottle of prosecco.

Christmas Tree

Christmas Tree at Apple Market

Festive Christmas tree set up & decorated in the apple market, adds a fun and festive feeling to the surrounding area.

Santa Post Box

Post box for Santa Claus Letters

Giant Christmas post box where children can post their letters to santa.
The post box is emptied every Saturday & Sunday at 4:30pm. Last collection 23rd Dec.

Ice Skating

Ice Skating at the Quay

Families and couples can have great fun with 55 minute sessions on the Ice.

Carousel

Carousel at Waterford Square

Wonderful carousel with a colourful display of lights and music designed for the fun of children.
